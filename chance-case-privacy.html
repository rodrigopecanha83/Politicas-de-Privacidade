Privacy Policy for the Extension:
"Chance Case Chrome Extension"

Last updated: November 12, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Google Chrome extension "Chance Case Chrome Extension" (hereinafter referred to as the "Extension") handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Use

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or browsing data of the user.

The functionality of the Extension is performed entirely within your local browser. It is designed for a single purpose: **to read the text saved on your clipboard and rewrite it to the same location with the chosen capitalization format (Capitalize, Title Case, UPPERCASE, or lowercase).** No information from your clipboard or browsing activity is sent to external servers or third parties.

2. Required Permissions

To function correctly, the Extension requests the following permissions in Google Chrome. Below, we explain why each one is necessary:

  • clipboardRead and clipboardWrite: These permissions are essential for the main function of the Extension. **clipboardRead** is required to read the text currently copied by the user. **clipboardWrite** is required to write the formatted text back to your operating system's clipboard.

3. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically to stay informed.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
